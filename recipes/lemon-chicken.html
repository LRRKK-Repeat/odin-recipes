Lemon Chicken

Description

Grilled Lemon Chicken. Quick, easy and absolutely delicious - what more can I say?

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 Cup of Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Ginger
  • 1/4 tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 4 Chicken Breast Halves

Steps

  1. Rinse chicken pieces, dabbing dry with a paper towel.
  2. Stir the lemon, ginger, pepper and soy together in a jug.
  3. Pour the mixture into a resealable plastic bag or container.
  4. Add the raw chicken before massaging it in and leaving it to marinate for up to 24h.
  5. Heat and oil your grill.
  6. Drain the juices away from the chicken before placing on the grill to cook until no longer pink inside.
  7. Enjoy with salad or fries!
