Pancakes

Description

Pancakes. Just like your mother used to make!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of All Purpose Flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp of Baking Powder
  • 1/4 tsp of Salt (or more)
  • 1 tbsp of White Sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups of Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 tbsp of Butter, melted

Steps

  1. Sift the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar together.
  2. Form a well in the middle of the dry mixture.
  3. Pour the milk, egg and melted butter into the well, mixing until smooth.
  4. Head a frying pan to medium heat.
  5. Pour a small plate sized portion of the mixture into the pan.
  6. Cook until you see small bubbles throughout, then flip.
  7. Cook until golden brown, then leave to stand for 1-2min(s).
  8. Serve with maple syrup, or your preferred alternative toppings.
  9. Enjoy!
